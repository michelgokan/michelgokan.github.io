Michel Gokan Khan

CTO, Technologist, Chairman, Founder, Entrepreneur
Stockholm, Sweden


Biography

I am a Stockholm-based technologist and entrepreneur. I currently serve as CTO of Co-Cart AB and co-founder of Softwarify (2025–Present), and previously worked as CTO of Spotin AB (2022–2024) as well as other hands-on tech leadership roles before that. My research spans ML-based optimization of cloud-native systems, digital twinning, and computer vision; industrially I focus on software design, architecture, and microservice-oriented development. I conducted research on digital twinning and optimization as a PostDoc at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in collaboration with AstraZeneca, and I hold a PhD in Machine Learning Driven Optimization of Cloud Native Systems from Karlstad University in Sweden. My publications include work on microservice performance simulation, modelling, and optimization (e.g., PerfSim), building on earlier research at Ericsson and other engineering leadership roles.
